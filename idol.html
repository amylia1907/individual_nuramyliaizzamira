My Idol

Vivy Yusof

My Idol of Entrepreneur Vivy Yusof is my idol. Vivy Yusof is the co-founder of The dUCk Group, which is one of the fastest growing local brands in Malaysia selling scarves, bags and cosmetics. dUCk has opened stores in Pavilion, KLCC, Setia City Mall, One Utama, IOI City Mall, KL East Mall and on Haji Lane in Singapore. Vivy has been featured in many local and international media platforms such as BBC, CNBC, The New York Times, VICE and Forbes. Vivy Yusof is a strong believer of the local fashion industry, but she is also recognised by international brands. Vivy Yusof was also one of the 100 influential ladies from across the globe to be a part of the #DiorSaddle campaign in 2018.
